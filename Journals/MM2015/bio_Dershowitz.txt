Nachum Dershowitz is a Professor of Computational Logic at Tel Aviv
University.  His graduate degrees in applied mathematics are from the
Weizmann Institute of Science.  He is coauthor of \emph{Calendrical
Calculations} (Cambridge University Press, 4th ed., forthcoming) and
\emph{Calendrical Tabulations} (Cambridge University Press, 2002) and
was elected to Academia Europaea in 2013.
